Sandeep Jain

Geeks for Geeks

Sandeep Jain
Sandeep Jain is the founder and CEO of GeeksForGeeks. Computer programming has become more or less a mandatory skill that all companies look for in a potential candidate. However, the outdated curriculum of most institutions has students resorting to other means like tuitions. Recognising the need of the hour, Sandeep Jain created the one-stop destination for all those looking to master coding - GeeksForGeeks. A computer science portal for coding enthusiasts, it contains extremely informative articles explaining programming concepts, practice quizzes and company interview/programming questions.
